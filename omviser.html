Your College Advisor

Overview

A platform that connects international students with educational advisers to guide them through the college admission process in the United States.

Role

UX/UI Design Intern

Timeline

June 2020 - August 2020

Team

Worked along 3 other designers, co-lead designer, and lead designer.

Context

omviser is a platform that connects international students with peer advisers and educational advisers to guide them through college (undergraduate and graduate) admissions processes in the United States. It is a system that gives the students the best opportunity to access higher education through the pairing of an adviser with experience studying in the U.S and their educational process.

The platform hopes to empower international students by providing location-agnostic support networks for and during their college admission and application process. The main goal of this project is to design a responsive site that makes mentorship more accessible, irrespective of socioeconomic conditions or geographic boundaries, and provide a platform to coach international students.

Challenge

Understanding users' needs and pain points in the college admissions process for most international students and building a responsive UI through research and design process.

Conclusion

Although my time at omviser is done, the UI/UX team plans to move forth with this final design iteration and perform usability testing to evaluate user feedback and enhance the platform. Usability testing would include a task-focused plan and would be evaluated using the following metrics to address both usability success and overall product success:

  1. Task Success Rate
  2. Time Taken Per Task
  3. Net Promoter Score
  4. Customer Satisfaction Scale

Bubbles is a retail brand specializing in children’s clothing. As a high-end destination for all things children, the visual identity explores the playful and innocence of a child juxtaposed with grown-up sophistication. The bubbles pattern is the hero in a joyful visual identity elevated with a vibrant yellow and a light blue applied throughout implementations. To embody these qualities, the identity heavily relies upon a vivid and elegant color palette and artifacts—rubber duck, wood geometric solids, and ribbons. The symbol is a nod to the definition of bubbles — from bubbles —and its initial B, and it can be paired with the logotype or detached to work as a secondary logo.

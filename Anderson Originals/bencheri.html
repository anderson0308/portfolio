BenCheri Educational Center is a non-profit organization. located in one of the at-risk and low-to-moderate income neighborhoods. The organization focuses on providing tutoring to kids in Math, Reading, STEM, Writing, and Art classes that will lead to a career pathway for our students to attend a local Community College or University. The center offers many resources to the community and ensure the Children receive the tools needed to SUCCEED.
As an educational center, the visual identity encompasses different subjects. The color palette - bright and colorful - helps to portray the positive work the center does.

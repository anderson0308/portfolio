BenCheri Educational Center

Bridge the gap of illiteracy among our youth

Overview

BenCheri Educational Center is a non-profit organization, located in one of the at-risk and low-to-moderate income neighborhoods. The organization focuses on providing tutoring to kids in Math, Reading, STEM, Writing, and Art classes that will lead to a career pathway for our students to attend a local Community College or University. The center offers many resources to the community and ensure the Children receive the tools needed to SUCCEED. I was responsible for the rebranding efforts of the center. Moreover, I designed promotional material and manage their social media presence.

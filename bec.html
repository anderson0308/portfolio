BenCheri Educational Center

One child at a time

Overview

BenCheri Educational Center is a non-profit organization, located in one of the at-risk and low-to-moderate income neighborhoods. The organization focuses on providing tutoring to kids in Math, Reading, STEM, Writing, and Art classes that will lead to a career pathway for our students to attend a local Community College or University. The center offers many resources to the community and ensure the Children receive the tools needed to SUCCEED. I was the opportunity to be the Marketing Manager. My responsibilities included, redesigning the center’s branding and UI material, updating their social media account and creating print material.
